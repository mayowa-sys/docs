---
title: "Proration"
description: "Fair billing when customers change plans mid-cycle"
---

When a customer switches plans mid-billing-cycle, RailSwitch calculates a fair adjustment: a **credit** for the unused portion of the current plan and a **charge** for the remaining days on the new plan.

## How It Works

```
Credit   = (Current Plan Amount / Total Days) × Remaining Days
Charge   = (New Plan Amount / Total Days) × Remaining Days
Net      = Charge - Credit
```

### Example

A customer on **Starter ($50/mo)** switches to **Pro ($150/mo)** with 5 days left in the billing period:

- Credit: $50 / 30 days × 5 remaining days = **$8.33** (unused Starter)
- Charge: $150 / 30 days × 5 remaining days = **$25.00** (prorated Pro)
- Net charge: **$16.67**

## Previewing a Proration

Before changing a plan, call the preview endpoint to see the breakdown:

```python
preview = client.subscriptions.preview(
    sub_id="sub_001",
    new_plan_id="plan_pro",
)
print(preview.net_amount)  # 1666.67
```

```javascript
const preview = await client.subscriptions.preview(
  "sub_001",
  "plan_pro",
);
console.log(preview.net_amount); // 1666.67
```
